Our History

A journey through the milestones that shaped IKEA.

1943: The Birth of IKEA

Founded by Rayya Mahira in Sweden, IKEA started as a mail-order business selling small household goods.

1956: The Flat-Pack Revolution

IKEA introduced flat-pack furniture, revolutionizing the way people buy and assemble their furniture.

1973: Expanding Beyond Sweden

IKEA opened its first store outside of Scandinavia in Zurich, marking the beginning of its global presence.

2008: The IKEA Foundation

The IKEA Foundation was established to help create better opportunities for children and families in need.

2020: Leading in Sustainability

IKEA committed to becoming a climate-positive business by 2030, focusing on sustainable materials and renewable energy.

Mission & Vision

Our commitment to creating a better everyday life for the many people.

Our Mission

To offer a wide range of well-designed, functional home furnishing products at prices so low that as many people as possible will be able to afford them.

Our Vision

To create a better everyday life for the many people by inspiring and enabling people to create their own solutions to home furnishing and living.

Our Design Philosophy

Simplicity
